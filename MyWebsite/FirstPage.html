前言

是的，这是一个我的oc（original character）网站，这是我能拿出来最丰富、能代表我的作品了。从2020年， 我高二时，第一个人物“屈邪”出现到现在，一直在不断更改添加，不知不觉已经变成了一个比较大的世界观了。他们记录了我这些年不同的精神状态，也 反映着我对世界认识对一些变化。不过不变对是，我依然想让“人与人间对真情”“理想主义者的反抗”作为创作的核心理念。所以当我发现自己需要给这一系列 故事取一个统一的名字来作为网站名称，我想到了“曙红时代”。我热爱红色，这是象征鲜血象征爱的颜色，而我希望它总是能有一线希望和曙光的，所以应当是 曙红这样一种颜色。总之，这是非常个人化的作品，也是非常个人化的网站，也许别人很难理解但我还是把它做出来了。希望以后能继续被他们鼓舞激励着， 让我在现实的生活中也能感受到这样的一股力量。

Yes, this is my oc (original character) website, which is as rich and representative of my work as I can bring out. From 2020, When I was a sophomore in high school, the first character "Qu evil" appeared until now, and has been constantly changing and adding, unconsciously it has become a relatively large world view. They've documented my different mental states over the years, too It reflects some changes in my understanding of the world. But the same thing is that I still want to make "people against the truth" and "the resistance of idealists" as the core concept of creation. So when I found myself needing to give this series Story to take a unified name as the name of the site, I think of "dawn era". I love red, which is the color of blood and love, and I hope it always has a glimmer of hope and light, so it should be Eosin is such a color. All in all, this is a very personal work and a very personal website, and it may be difficult for others to understand but I still made it. I hope to continue to be inspired by them in the future, Let me in real life can also feel such a force.
